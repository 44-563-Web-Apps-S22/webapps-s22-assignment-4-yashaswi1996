MUSIC MASTER:

Do you wish to see our pieces?

.MONSIEUR-JOURDAIN{
MONSIEUR JOURDAIN:

Yes.

MUSIC MASTER:

I have already told you that this is a little attempt

I have made to show the different passions that music can express.

MONSIEUR JOURDAIN:

Very good.

MUSIC MASTER:

(To musicians)

Here, come forward.

(To Monsieur Jourdain)

You must imagine that they are dressed as shepherds.

MONSIEUR JOURDAIN:

Why always as shepherds? You see nothing but that everywhere.

MUSIC MASTER:

Without doubt. You must have three voices--a tenor, a soprano, and a bass, who will be accompanied by a bass-viol, a theorbo, and a clavecin for the chords, with two violins to play the ritournelles.

MONSIEUR JOURDAIN:

You must also add a trumpet marine. The trumpet marine is an instrument that pleases me and it's harmonious.
