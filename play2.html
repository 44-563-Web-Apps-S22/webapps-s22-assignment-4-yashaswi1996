MONSIEUR JOURDAIN:

Very good.

MUSIC MASTER:

(To musicians)

Here, come forward.

MUSIC MASTER:

Without doubt. You must have three voices--a tenor, a soprano, and a bass, who will be accompanied by a bass-viol, a theorbo, and a clavecin for the chords, with two violins to play the ritournelles.
